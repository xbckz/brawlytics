About Brawlytics

Your comprehensive Brawl Stars competitive analytics platform

What is Brawlytics?

Brawlytics is an advanced statistics and analytics platform designed specifically for competitive Brawl Stars players and teams. It tracks matches, analyzes performance, and provides information about the brawler meta, player stats, team compositions, and more.

The platform uses the official Brawl Stars API to collect all of the data displayed, making it 100% accurate and up to date.

Understanding the Data

Meta Score Calculation

The meta score combines win rate and pick rate to identify the most dominant brawlers:

Meta Score = Win Rate × Pick Rate

This ensures that brawlers with both high win rates and frequent picks are ranked appropriately.

Tier List Generation

Tier lists are automatically generated using percentile-based distribution:

  • S Tier: Top 10% of brawlers
  • A Tier: Next 20% (10-30%)
  • B Tier: Next 30% (30-60%)
  • C Tier: Next 25% (60-85%)
  • D Tier: Next 10% (85-95%)
  • F Tier: Bottom 5%

Synergy & Matchups

Brawlytics tracks which brawlers work well together (teammates) and which brawlers counter each other (matchups) based on actual match outcomes. Minimum sample sizes ensure statistical reliability.

FAQ

How accurate is the data?

The data is 100% accurate as it comes directly from the official Brawl Stars API. Only friendly games are tracked to ensure consistency and reliability in competitive statistics.

How often are stats updated?

Statistics are collected in real time. The dashboard uses caching (5 minute refresh) for optimal performance while keeping data up to date.

How are tracked players added?

Players are added to the suggested list after playing with already tracked players. They must then be manually approved before tracking begins. This process ensures that all worthy players from every region are included.

Please report any issues through Discord. I continuously try to improve the platform based on user feedback.
